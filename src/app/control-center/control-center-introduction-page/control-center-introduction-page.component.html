A short Introduction

Welcome to Cryptic. Cryptic is a hacker browsergame. Within this project, the further education and training of people
interested in computer science is promoted through the development of a game. And this is the game we are talking about.

You could start the game, with building your first device, by clicking the big green Button. After that, you can connect
to your device by clicking the blue screen.

The next step could be a wallet. You need a wallet for many things. If you hacked a device of another player you can rob
him, and steal all his morphcoin(The ingame currency), or you can use the miner app to mine your own morphcoin. You see,
these are only two possibilities in a whole world so, what are you waiting for. Start your device and become the best
player. Gain access over tons of devices and build your hacking empire.

You can reach this text by clicking the introduction tab in the control center on the left side.
If you want to learn more about cryptic you could read in our wiki.
